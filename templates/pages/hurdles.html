Jumping Events


Jumping Events: Jumping events include hurdling, long jump, and high jump. Sprinting fast is important for hurdling, and long jump, but you can still be good without much speed. People who are taller and have high hops generally choose these events, but any height is acceptable. If you like jumping high or far, than these events are perfect for you.
